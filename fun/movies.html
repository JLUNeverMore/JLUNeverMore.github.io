Movies

Here I list some of my favorite movies.
  • The Shawshank Redemption(肖申克的救赎). Director: Frank Darabont, Writers: Stephen King, Stars: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman, Bob Gunton. The body may be locked away in a cell, the spirit can never be truly imprisoned.
  • Forrest Gump(阿甘正传). Director: Robert Zemeckis, Stars: Tom Hanks, Robin Wright. Life was like a box of chocolates, you never know what you're going to get.
  • The Dark Kngit(蝙蝠侠:黑暗骑士). Director: Christopher Nolan, Stars: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger. The film is the second part of Nolan's The Dark Knight Trilogy, and I think is the best part.
  • Interstellar(星际穿越). Director: Christopher Nolan, Stars: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway. A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival.
  • The Martain(火星救援). Director: Ridley Scott, Stars: Matt Damon. An astronaut becomes stranded on Mars after his team assume him dead, and must rely on his ingenuity to find a way to signal to Earth that he is alive.
  • The Shining(闪灵). Director: Stanley Kubrick, Writer: Stephen King, Stars: Jack Nicholson. This is my favorite horror films.

Biography

I am a graduate student of Department of Computer Science & Technology in Nanjing University and a member of LAMDA Group, which is led by professor Zhi-Hua Zhou.

I received my B.Sc. degree from Software College of Jilin University. In September 2017, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc. degree in Nanjing University.

Research Interests

My research interests include Machine Learning and Data Mining, expecially on weakly supervised learning, e.g., semi-supervised learning, transfer learning and so on.
